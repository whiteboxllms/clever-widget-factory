# Spec Workflow Rules

## Requirements Phase — MANDATORY

When creating or updating requirements documents for specs:

1. **DO NOT delegate requirements writing to a subagent.** Write requirements yourself, directly.
2. **Present ONE requirement at a time** to the user for approval before writing the next.
3. **Wait for explicit user approval** ("looks good", "yes", "approved", etc.) before adding the requirement to the document and moving to the next one.
4. **Do NOT generate the entire requirements document in one shot.** Each requirement must be discussed and approved individually.
5. If the user provides feedback on a requirement, revise it and re-present for approval before moving on.

## Design and Tasks Phases

Design and tasks phases may be delegated to subagents since they derive from approved requirements.

## General

- The user's approval is the ground truth. Never assume approval.
- When in doubt, ask before writing.
